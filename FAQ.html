FAQ

When does the application period open?
The application runs from the 1st of September to the 31st of October every year.
What is the turnround period after applications have been submitted?
Application outcomes are emailed to parents within 7 to 30 working days after applications have been received.
When can parents enroll children for extra mural activities?
As soon as the learners receive confirmation and have been accepted for the academic year.
How much is the application fee?
The application fee for grade 1 to grade 7 is R100 and for grade 8 to grade 12 it's R150.
Is there an open period for walk-ins and late applications?
The school does not provide for late applications and walk-ins.
